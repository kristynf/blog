HTML/CSS

by Kristyn Ferber, 02/14/2020



HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap are complicated only by the sheer volume options and the subsequent amount of exponential possibilities for customization beyond the basics. It is also much more forgiving than Java or even Python. A website will load even if there are mistakes, perhaps not well and maybe not fully functional, but it will load. For this reason, my learning has been more relaxed.

However, one part I have not enjoyed is the design aspect. I am not very artsy. That seems to have skipped a generation. For that reason, I do wish we could spend more time on the more "intellectually" challenging Java, where more practice will lead to more skill. :)
